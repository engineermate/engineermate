Darcy-Weisbach formula is based on theoretical analysis by using experimental data to deal with the actual engineering calculation. It can resolve the lost head of any fluid flowing in pipes and conduits. Since more than one and half centuries ago, numerous scientists and engineers in hydraulics have been contributed their work to make the use of formula practical.

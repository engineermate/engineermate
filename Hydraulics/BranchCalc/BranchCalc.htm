Water Source Calculation

The water source calculation will be performed after entering the flow testing data.

Firstly, the K factor will be calculated based on the data, which were entered. It is an indicator of the piping system water distribution capacity, independing from water flow and pressure.

It is well known that the flow test may not be conducted during the most unfavorable time of the day. Therefore, a safety margin factor SF is introduced to take into account the unpredicted conditions as well as the future piping deterioration and development, etc.

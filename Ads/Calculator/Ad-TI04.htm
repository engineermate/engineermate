BottmL (tranch length): 100 ft

Tranch side slope: L:H=0.5
Note:
Suppose L/H is the same for tranch sides and the ends.
Fig. 4.1.1-1 Excovation Input Data
BotW/BotL/Depth(ft)
3    100  10

ExcavatVolume(cyd):

Fig.4.1-1-2 shows the results:
The 1st and 2nd lines show the bottom width (BotW), bottom length (BotL) and depth of the trench.

The excavation volume is 296 cubic yard, excluding the end slope, and 314 cubic yard if the slope at both ends is considered.

Note: The volume of the excavation is converted to cubic yard (cye). For metric system, unit can be meter and cubic meter.

Select 4, Pump-HP, on Fig.3.0.1 to bring the Pump Horse Power screen.

Q(gpm): 750
Hd(ft): 200
  E(%): 65





A. Enter:
Enter data as on Fig.3.4-1:
Flow Q=750 gpm
Boost head Hd=200 ft
Pump efficiency E=65%
Fig. 3.4-1 Enter Data


   HP: 58.30

Affinity Law:
q/Q=n/N=d/D
h/H=(n/N)^2=(d/D)^2
p/P=(n/N)^3=(d/D)^3
B. Output and notes:
HP = 58.30

The Affinity Laws follow:
q and Q - flow before and after;
n and N - revolutions before and after;
d and d - diameter of impeller before and after;
p and P - pump power required before and after.

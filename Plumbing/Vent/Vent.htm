Trouble Shooting

Calculations on some pages are dependent on other data file(s) being loaded. In this case, WAITE FOR THE PAGE TO BE FULLY LOADED. Otherwise, calculation may be terminated and error message may appear.

Venting

How to Use:

  1. Select waste stack size previously sized. (Waite for the page to be fully loaded).
  2. Enter DFU.
  3. Enter TDL.
  4. The recommended vent diameter will show in the cell.
  5. The maximum DFU and TDL for the vent size you selected will show in the last two cells.
  6. Notes:
    • Vent stack or stack vent size is dependent on DFU and TDL, but other requirements may apply, such as aggregate venting, minimum size of vent terminal, etc.
    • If cells for Vent Dia, Max DFU and Max TDL are blank, it means that the waste stack size may be too small.

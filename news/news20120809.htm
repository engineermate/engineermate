美国在耗能效率方面排第几？

Ninth out of the 12 major world economies, according to the Inter2012 national Energy Efficiency Scorecard（阅读原文） by the American Council for an Energy-Efficient Economy. The United States has made "limited or little progress toward greater efficiency at the national level" in the past decade, says the report, putting it well behind the United Kingdom, Germany, and Italy, which scored first, second, and third respectively.

非住宅建筑消费估计在2013年增加6.2%

The American Institute of Architects' semi-annual Consensus Construction Forecast（阅读原文） projects a 4.4 percent rise in spending for the remainder of this year for nonresidential construction projects due to a spike in demand for industrial facilities, and spending is expected to increase even more next year.

ASSE加入IAPMO等待投票

IAPMO President Dan Daniels and CEO GP Russ Chaney formally presented an invitation for the transition Friday, July 20, to the American Society of Sanitary Engineering board of directors, which accepted the invitation. IAPMO and ASSE are now working to draft a legally binding document for the transition, which will be balloted to ASSE's membership at its annual meeting this November. More>>（阅读原文）

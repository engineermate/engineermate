生产一瓶可口可乐需要用多少水？

The Coca-Cola Company and The Nature Conservancy recently performed water footprint studies to determine how much water is used to produce the company’s products. A product water footprint is the total volume of freshwater consumed, directly and indirectly, to produce a product. According to the results, 35 liters of water are used to produce one 0.5-liter bottle of Coke. For more information on water footprints, visit the Water Footprint Network’s website.

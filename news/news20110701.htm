纳米超级砂用于净水

Researchers at Rice University have used nanotechnology to create coated sand that has enhanced properties for water purification. In tests of water contaminated with 400 parts per billion (ppb) of mercury, the coated sand absorbed the metal for one hour and resulted in filtered water with less than 1 ppb of mercury. （阅读原文）
